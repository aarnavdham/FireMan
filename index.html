How to Play

Objective

Guide every active hero to their matching elemental exit door. Collect gems for bonus score. Avoid hazards that harm your element.

Elements

  • FIRE Safe in lava, melts ice, burns wood obstacles.
  • WATER Safe in water, extinguishes fire, buoyant in pools.
  • EARTH Heavy — presses heavy plates, breaks weak floors.
  • ICE Slippery, freezes water into temporary platforms.
  • WIND Glides on air currents, activates wind switches.

Controls

Each player has Move / Jump / Action / Sprint keys. Hold Sprint while moving to run faster.

Player 1 — Fire

Move: AD · Jump: W/Space · Action: S · Sprint: L-Shift

Player 2 — Water

Move: ←→ · Jump: ↑ · Action: ↓ · Sprint: R-Shift

Player 3 — Earth

Move: JL · Jump: I · Action: K · Sprint: U

Player 4 — Ice

Move: Num4Num6 · Jump: Num8 · Action: Num5 · Sprint: Num0

Player 5 — Wind

Move: TY · Jump: G · Action: H (hold=glide) · Sprint: R

Gamepad

D-pad/Stick move · A jumps · X acts · Left Bumper sprints

Online Multiplayer

• Enter your name at the top of the lobby — it shows above your character in-game!
• Click Multiplayer → Host tab → click Create Room.
• Share that 6-character code with friends.
• Friends click Multiplayer → switch to the Join Room tab → enter the code → click Join.
• Each player uses only their own control scheme (Player 1 = Fire, Player 2 = Water, etc.).
• The host runs the game authoritatively; clients send inputs and receive state. Client-side prediction keeps your own movement smooth.
• Requires an internet connection for the initial P2P handshake (PeerJS). Falls back to local co-op if offline.

Tips

• Coyote time (0.16s) lets you jump shortly after leaving a ledge — forgiving platforming.
• Jump buffering (0.18s) remembers your press — queue jumps before landing.
• Hold jump for higher hops; tap for short hops.
• Sprint to cross wide gaps and outrun hazards.
• Earth's weight can crack fragile floors — drop from height!
• Wind can glide across huge gaps by holding the action key.
